Privacy Policy

Your Trust, Honored

Plain language. No tricks. Here's exactly how The Oracle handles your information.

Information We Collect

To provide your readings and improve the experience, The Oracle collects:

  • Palm photos you choose to upload for a reading.
  • Device information such as device model, operating system version, and app version.
  • Usage data including which features you use and how often, used solely to improve the app.

How Your Palm Photos Are Used

Palm photos are securely transmitted to Anthropic, our AI processing partner, where they are analyzed to generate your personalized reading. Photos are not permanently stored after the reading is generated. Once your reading is complete, the image is discarded from our processing pipeline.

Subscriptions & Payments

The Oracle uses Apple in-app purchases for all subscription billing. We never see or store your payment details — Apple handles all transactions in accordance with its own privacy practices.

We Do Not Sell Your Data
